## 三、通用提示词模板

当你需要生成更多同居素材时，可以套用以下模板：

### 模板：[角色] + [场景] + [光线] + [风格]

```
A small crochet amigurumi [角色描述] doll, [姿势/动作], 
in a real [场景] setting. [光线描述], shallow depth of field, 
[风格描述]. Lifestyle photography, warm tones.
```

### 角色描述速查

| 角色 | 英文描述 |
|------|----------|
| 羊绅士 | sheep doll, cream white yarn texture, wearing a tiny brown top hat, holding a miniature walking cane, round body, black bead eyes |
| 莓莓 | strawberry doll, red body with green leaf top, very short stubby legs, round body, black bead eyes, calm expression |
| 啾啾 | bird doll, grey and blue yarn, round head, wings folded flat against body, black bead eyes, energetic posture |

### 场景速查

| 场景 | 英文关键词 |
|------|-----------|
| 咖啡杯旁 | next to a real coffee cup on wooden desk |
| 键盘上 | standing on a computer keyboard |
| 窗台上 | sitting on a windowsill, looking outside |
| 书旁边 | next to an open book |
| 枕头上 | on a white pillow |
| 水果盘里 | among real fruits in a bowl |
| 鞋旁边 | next to real shoes on the floor |
| 花丛中 | among real flowers in a garden |

### 光线速查

| 光线 | 英文关键词 |
|------|-----------|
| 早晨 | warm morning sunlight from the side |
| 午后 | soft afternoon light |
| 傍晚 | golden hour sunset light, warm orange tones |
| 阴天 | soft diffused overcast light |
| 室内 | warm indoor ambient light |

## 四、Midjourney 批量生成技巧

1. 用 `/imagine` 逐条输入提示词
2. 选定一张满意的图后，用 `--sref [图片URL]` 保持风格一致
3. 生成后用 `--cref [图片URL]` 保持角色一致
4. 建议先生成羊绅士单张，确认风格后，用这张做参考生成其余图片

### 风格锁定示例
```
/imagine [你的提示词] --sref [第一张满意的图片URL] --cref [羊绅士参考图URL] --ar 3:4 --v 6
```
